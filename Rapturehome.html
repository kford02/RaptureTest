Rapture represents a state of intense pleasure and joy which is exactly what Rapture Events Boston provides. We are a unique event planning and promotions company focused on consolidating entrepreneurial, musical, and artistic markets while partnering with grass roots service organizations to uplift Boston communities.

Services

We electrify metro Boston by providing an outlet for art, entertainment, and creativity that caters to an urban market.

We create mutually beneficial partnerships between professional organizations and Boston’s diverse urban consumers.

We dedicate a minimum of 10% of proceeds to Boston’s urban communities in need. Fighting homelessness. Combating Hunger. Affecting change.

We find creative ways to fundraise in support of campaigns focused on hunger (ex. Fill the Fridge), homelessness, and health/fitness.
